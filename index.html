    Taco Trivia

    When did Tacos first appears in the United State?

    Tacos made their way into the United States in the late 19th century. Mexican immigrants brought this delicious dish across the border, introducing it to the American Southwest. As these immigrants settled in places like Texas and California, they started selling tacos from stands or trucks, gradually popularizing them across the country.
